Rerouting Network of The Benjamin Banneker Building

  • Our task was to reroute a the network for the Benjamin Banneker building. We had to assess the current network infrastructure, identify any weaknesses or limitations, and determine the requirements for the new network. This included considering factors such as network speed, security, scalability, and the number of devices that will be connected.
